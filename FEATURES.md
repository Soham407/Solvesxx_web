# FacilityPro - Features Overview

This document lists all the features available in the FacilityPro platform, organized by user type. Each feature is explained in simple terms so you can understand what it does and how it helps.

---

## Admin Features

These features are available to system administrators who manage the entire platform.

### Dashboard Hub

A central control panel where you can see important numbers and statistics at a glance. You can switch between 12 different views based on your role - whether you're a Managing Director, Department Head, Accountant, or any other stakeholder. Each view shows information relevant to that role.

### Employee Directory

A complete list of all employees in your organization. You can:

- Add new employees to the system
- View and update employee information
- Search and filter employees by name, department, or role
- Download the employee list as a spreadsheet (CSV file)

### Leave Management

Review and manage time-off requests from your staff. When an employee asks for leave (sick leave, casual leave, etc.), you can:

- See all pending requests in one place
- Check how many leave days the employee has remaining
- Approve or reject requests with one click
- View the history of past leave applications

### Asset Registry

Keep track of all equipment, machinery, and valuable items owned by your organization. You can:

- Add new assets with details like purchase date, location, and condition
- Generate QR code stickers for each asset (for easy scanning and identification)
- See which assets are working, under repair, or need attention
- Track where each asset is located

### Work Order Management (Service Requests)

Create and track maintenance or repair requests. When something needs fixing or servicing:

- Create a new request describing the problem
- Assign it to the right person or team
- Track progress from "Open" to "In Progress" to "Completed"
- Link requests to specific assets (e.g., "AC unit in Room 101 needs repair")

### Product Inventory

Manage your stock of supplies and materials. The system:

- Shows current stock levels for all products
- Alerts you when items are running low (Low Stock warning)
- Tells you when items are completely out (Out of Stock alert)
- Helps you keep track of what needs to be reordered

### Security Command Center

Monitor your security guards in real-time. This feature shows:

- Live GPS location of each guard on a map
- Whether guards are inside their assigned area (geo-fence)
- Guard's phone battery level and device status
- Current shift status (on duty, break, off duty)
- Alerts when a guard leaves their assigned area without permission

### Visitor Log

Track all visitors entering and exiting your premises. You can:

- See who is currently inside the building
- Record entry and exit times automatically
- Manage daily helpers and regular visitors separately
- Quickly check out visitors when they leave

### Panic Alert Response Center

Handle emergency situations when residents or staff press the SOS/panic button:

- See all active emergency alerts on a map
- View the exact GPS location of the person in distress
- Access their emergency contact information
- Mark alerts as "Responding" or "Resolved"
- View history of past emergencies

## Employee Features

These features are available to regular employees of the organization.

### Leave Applications

Request time off from work easily:

- Submit leave requests (sick leave, casual leave, earned leave, etc.)
- See how many leave days you have remaining for each type
- Track the status of your requests (Pending, Approved, Rejected)
- View your leave history

### Attendance Check-in

Mark your attendance when you arrive at and leave work:

- Check-in using GPS location (proves you're at the workplace)
- Take a selfie for verification (optional, based on company policy)
- See your attendance history and working hours
- View if your check-in was verified or flagged

---

## Security Guard Features

These features are designed for security personnel.

### Visitor Management

Process visitors at the gate:

- Record visitor details (name, purpose, who they're meeting)
- Note vehicle information if they're driving
- Mark visitors as "Checked In" when they enter
- Mark visitors as "Checked Out" when they leave
- Handle pre-approved visitors (invited by residents)

### Panic Alert Response

Respond to emergency alerts from residents:

- Receive instant notifications when someone presses SOS
- See the exact location of the emergency
- View contact details of the person and their emergency contacts
- Update the alert status as you respond

---

## Resident Features

These features are available to residents living in a managed society or apartment complex.

### My Flat Portal

Your personal dashboard for managing your flat:

- View your flat details and registered family members
- See your registered vehicles
- Access society announcements and updates
- Quick access to important features

### Invite Visitors

Pre-authorize your guests before they arrive:

- Send a visitor invitation with expected date and time
- Share a digital pass with your guest (they can show it at the gate)
- Guards will know your visitor is expected and allow quick entry
- Cancel invitations if plans change

### Emergency SOS Button

Get help quickly in an emergency:

- Press the panic button to alert security immediately
- Your exact GPS location is shared automatically
- Security guards receive instant notification
- Your emergency contacts are displayed to responders
